The Elden Wiki

The Golden Order has been broken.

Rise, Tarnished, and be guided by grace to brandish the power of the Elden Ring and become an Elden Lord in the Lands Between.

In the Lands Between ruled by Queen Marika the Eternal, the Elden Ring, the source of the Erdtree, has been shattered.

Marika's offspring, demigods all, claimed the shards of the Elden Ring known as the Great Runes, and the mad taint of their newfound strength triggered a war: The Shattering. A war that meant abandonment by the Greater Will.

And now the guidance of grace will be brought to the Tarnished who were spurned by the grace of gold and exiled from the Lands Between. Ye dead who yet live, your grace long lost, follow the path to the Lands Between beyond the foggy sea to stand before the Elden Ring.

And become the Elden Lord.

Featured Lore

The Erdtree

The Erdtree is a towering, radiant tree and the divine symbol of order in the Lands Between. It channels the power of the Elden Ring and the Greater Will, its golden light blessing all beings—until the shattering disrupted its grace.

Queen Marika

Marika is the divine ruler chosen by the Greater Will. She shattered the Elden Ring in an act that changed the fate of the world. Her children, demigods, battled for its fragments, plunging the Lands Between into chaos.

Tarnished

The Tarnished, You, are exiled warriors once blessed by grace but now forsaken. After the Shattering, they are called back to reclaim their lost glory, gather the Great Runes, and attempt to become Elden Lord by restoring the Elden Ring.

Legendary Characters

Malenia, Blade of Miquella

A cursed yet elegant warrior unmatched in battle. Her rot corrodes everything—even herself.
